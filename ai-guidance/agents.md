# AI Agent Workflow

During the development of Refract, I utilized AI in three distinct roles:

1. **The Lead Architect:** I used the AI to design the initial relational database schema. We iterated on the relationship between 'Interviews' and 'Insights' to ensure data integrity.
2. **The Feature Engineer:** I used the AI to generate the boilerplate for the Flask routes and the React state management. This allowed me to focus on the high-level logic and integration.
3. **The QA Reviewer:** Before finalizing the code, I asked the AI to perform a 'Security and Logic Audit.' This led to the implementation of database transaction rollbacks and better CORS error handling.
